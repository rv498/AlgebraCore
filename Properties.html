Definition: Any number is equal to itself.
Definition: If first number is equal to second number, then second number is equal to first number.
Definition: If first number is equal to second number and second number is equal to third number, then first number is equal to third number.
Definition: When adding or multiplying, order of numbers is irrelevant.
Definition: When adding or multiplying, the way they are grouped together makes no difference in the outcome.
Definition: a number outside of grouped terms will multiply with each term in that group that are linked by addition or subtraction symbols.
Definition: When zero is added to any number, the outcome is that number unchanged.
Definition: When number one gets multiplied with the term, the outcome is just that term unchanged.
